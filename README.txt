=========================================
      Downloads Sorter v1.1.2
=========================================

Thank you for using Downloads Sorter! This tool automatically organizes your 
cluttered Downloads folder into neat categories.

--- INSTALLATION ---
1. Extract ALL files from this ZIP folder to a new folder on your computer.
2. Double-click 'Installer.exe' to begin.
3. (Optional) Enable 'Auto-Cleanup' to run every 6 hours (6am, 12pm, 6pm, 12am).

--- IMPORTANT: ANTIVIRUS WARNINGS ---
Windows and Antivirus programs may flag this as "Unknown."
Click "More info" and "Run anyway" or add an exclusion to Norton.

--- FEATURES ---
- Automatic sorting and 14-day temporary file cleanup.
- Resilient background scheduling.
- Custom branded desktop shortcut.

Enjoy your clean Downloads folder!
